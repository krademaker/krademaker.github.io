## Peer-reviewed publications

L. Dauban, M. Eder, M. de Haas, V.H. Franceschini-Santos, J.O. Yañez-Cuna, M. Martinovic, T. van Schaik, C. Leemans, H. Teunissen, **K. Rademaker**, M. Martinez Ara, M. Verkuilen, E. de Wit, B. van Steensel. “Interactions between the genome and the nuclear lamina are multivalent and cooperative”. _Nature Structural & Molecular Biology_, doi: [10.1038/s41594-025-01655-w](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41594-025-01655-w) (17 July 2025).
> In this paper, lamina-associated domains were scrambled using transposon local hopping to create deletions and inversions spanning LADs and flanks. Mapping of subsequent nuclear lamina interactions revealed multivalent and cooperative contacts between LADs and NL. <i class="fab fa-github"></i> <a href="https://github.com/lisedauban/LAD_scrambling"> Github repo (manuscript reproducibility)</a> <i class="fab fa-github"></i> <a href="https://github.com/krademaker/tagmapAnalyseR"> Github repo (transposon integration mapping)</a>


M. Olislagers, **K. Rademaker**, R. A. H. Adan, B. D. Lin & J. J. Luykx. 'Comprehensive analyses of RNA-seq and genome-wide data point to enrichment of neuronal cell type subsets in neuropsychiatric disorders'. _Molecular Psychiatry_ (27), p947–955, doi: [10.1038/s41380-021-01324-6](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41380-021-01324-6) (2022).
> In this paper, we mapped mouse neuronal cell type gene expression profiles to human orthologs, and conducted robust statistical testing to identify SNPs from 42 neuropsychiatric, substance use and behavioral/quantitative brain-related traits with enrichment in brain cell types. <i class="fab fa-github"></i> <a href="https://github.com/mitchellolislagers/cell_type_enrichment_pipeline"> Github repo </a>
